Coffee Shop API

Welcome to the Coffee Shop API. The following endpoints are available:

Get Special Coffee Products

GET /products-coffee.php

Get Special Dessert Products

GET /products-desserts.php

Get Coffee Products

GET /products.php?category=coffee

Get Dessert Products

GET /products.php?category=dessert

Get Testimonials

GET /testimonials.php

Get Product by ID

GET /product.php?id={id}
